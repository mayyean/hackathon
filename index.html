Hwang Hyunjin

Dancer, rapper, and singer recognized as a member of Stray Kids, a K-pop boy band which debuted on a 2018 reality show of the same name. His full name is Hwang Hyunjin.

Skills

Dancing

Hyunjin is Stray Kids' dancer for a reason. He is particularly known for his dancing skills. Often recognized as having such a powerful stage presence, Hyunjin makes the stage his playground. He looks the happiest every time he's on stage and makes it seems that he was born to perform.

Modeling

As the group's visual, it is no surprise to see Hyunjin across a variety of editorial pieces. With his stunning visuals and whatever style, he will certainly go viral amongst not only fans but also fellow idols; Hyunjin is unstoppable. Similar to his dances and artworks, Hyunjin expresses himself so well that you can feel it through the screen.

Photography

If we have #hyunpic for his art, we also have #hyuncam for all the beautiful photos Hyunjin personally takes and shares with fans. He is also known to carry a camera wherever he goes and would capture many cool street photos. According to his latest vlog on [SKZ VLOG] Hyunjin : Hyun.e's Holiday 5, he usually takes photos as inspirations for when he goes back to painting.
